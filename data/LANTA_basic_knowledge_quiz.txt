LANTA BASIC KNOWLEDGE QUIZ

Purpose: This quiz provides guidance on whether you should attend the
Introduction to LANTA HPC workshop. Workshop link:
https://thaisc.atlassian.net/wiki/x/R4DAKQ

Instructions: - This is an ungraded quiz. - You may select “do not know”
if you are unsure. - If your score is less than 9 out of 12, it is
recommended to attend the workshop.

Contact: thaisc-support@nstda.or.th

QUESTIONS

1)  Select all space on LANTA that you can use as a LANTA user. (1
    point)
    -   User home (/home/username)
    -   Project home (/project/ltxxxxxx-yyyy)
    -   Scratch (/scratch/ltxxxxxx-yyyy)
    -   Share folder (/project/share)
    -   do not know

    (Priority) reason. What does it mean? (1 point)

        queue.

        than the system pre-defined cut-off value.

        later.

    -   do not know

    -   do not know
